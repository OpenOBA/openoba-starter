3. **Originality Warranty** — You warrant that your contribution is original and does not infringe third-party rights. If you are employed, you confirm employer authorization.

4. **Dual Licensing** — The Company may distribute your contribution under open source (MIT / BSL 1.1) or commercial licenses.

5. **Withdrawal** — You may withdraw your contribution with 90 days' written notice. Previously distributed versions are unaffected. Withdrawal applies only to contributions that have not yet been merged into the OpenOBA main branch (master/main). Merged contributions are irrevocable, and the patent license granted under Article 2 is not affected by withdrawal under any circumstances.
