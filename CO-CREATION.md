## Three Ecosystems. Three Ways to Build and Earn.

### Ecosystem 1: ERDL Skeletons — The System Store for Industry Knowledge

Every industry has its secret language. Eyewear has prescriptions, lenses, pupillary distance. Healthcare has diagnoses, treatments, insurance codes. Logistics has routes, manifests, customs declarations.

An **ERDL Skeleton** captures this domain knowledge as executable system assets:

```yaml
entity: Prescription
  alias: ["处方", "Rx"]
  fields:
    - name: sph
      alias: ["球镜", "近视度数", "SPH"]
      type: decimal
      rule: between -20.00 and 20.00
    - name: cyl
      alias: ["柱镜", "散光度数"]
      type: decimal
      rule: between -10.00 and 0
```

**What you do**: Define the entities, rules, aliases, and actions for an industry you know inside-out.

**What you earn**: Each ERDL Skeleton is a package. Enterprises download it. Commercial use requires a license. **You set your price. You keep the majority.**

| Tier | You Earn | OpenOBA Takes |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Free / Community | Tips, consulting leads, reputation | 0% |
| Commercial License | **70%** of every license sale | 30% (platform + infrastructure) |
| Enterprise Custom | Your contract, your price | 0% (direct deal) |

**Like an App Store. But for systems, not apps.**

### Ecosystem 2: Skills — The System Store for AI Capabilities

A Skill is a reusable tool that the AI Executor can invoke: "Generate a sales report", "Validate inventory levels", "Sync with external CRM".

Skills are TypeScript modules registered in the OpenOBA Skill system. Once registered, any enterprise using OpenOBA can enable them with one click.

**What you do**: Build a Skill that solves a real business problem. The AI calls it. The Action Guard validates it. The enterprise benefits.

**What you earn**: Same model as ERDL Skeletons.

| Tier | You Earn | OpenOBA Takes |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Free / Community | Reputation, community contributions | 0% |
| Commercial License | **70%** per license | 30% |
| Custom Integration | Your consulting rate | 0% |

Real examples from our early ecosystem:
- **Report Generator** — Natural language → formatted Excel/PDF reports
- **Inventory Optimizer** — Predicts stock levels based on sales patterns
- **Compliance Auditor** — Scans ERDL rules against actual system state

### Ecosystem 3: Enterprise Implementation — The System Store for Solutions

Every enterprise that adopts OpenOBA needs implementation: ERDL setup, Skill configuration, workflow design, team training.

If you're a developer or consultant who knows a specific industry, this is your market. OpenOBA connects enterprises with implementers.

**What you do**: Deploy, configure, customize, and support OpenOBA for enterprise clients.

**What you earn**: Your consulting rate. 100%. OpenOBA takes nothing from implementation revenue. We only succeed when you succeed.
