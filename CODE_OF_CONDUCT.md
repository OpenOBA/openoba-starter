## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Being patient with non-native speakers (both English and Chinese)

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Hate speech (racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, religious discrimination)
- Persistent sealioning
- Impersonating OpenOBA officials, sponsors, or partners
- Using Issues/PRs for commercial advertising or solicitation

---

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and will take appropriate corrective action in response to violations.

### Enforcement Levels

| Level | Action | Duration |
|-------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Private warning + content retraction | One-time |
| 2 | Public warning + temporary mute | 30-90 days |
| 3 | Permanent mute + public notice | Permanent |
| 4 | Removal from project + contribution refusal | Permanent |

### Appeals

Sanctioned individuals may submit a written appeal via **postmaster@openoba.com** within 30 days of receiving the sanction notice. An independent review panel of at least 3 senior community members unrelated to the case will conduct the review. The review decision is final.

---

## Reporting

Report violations to: **postmaster@openoba.com**

Please include:
- Violator's GitHub username or identity
- Time, platform, and link (Issue/PR/comment URL)
- Screenshots or text copies of violating content
- Any witnesses
- Your contact information (anonymous reports accepted)

All reports will be:
- Acknowledged within 24 hours
- Assessed within 7 business days
- Resolved within 30 days

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all OpenOBA community spaces (GitHub repos, Discussions, official Discord/Slack, meetups, official social media) and when representing OpenOBA in public or private spaces.

## 我们的承诺

我们作为社区成员、贡献者和领导者，承诺让每个人在我们的社区中的参与体验免于骚扰，无论年龄、体型、残疾、族裔、性别特征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性认同与取向。

## 我们的标准

### 积极行为

- 使用包容和欢迎的语言
- 尊重不同的观点和经验
- 优雅地接受建设性批评
- 专注于对社区最有利的事务
- 对其他社区成员表现出同理心
- 对非母语使用者（包括英语和中文）保持耐心

### 不可接受的行为

- 使用性暗示的语言或图像，以及任何形式的性关注和挑逗
- 挑衅、侮辱或贬损性评论，人身攻击或政治攻击
- 公开或私下的骚扰行为
- 未经明确许可发布他人的私人信息
- 仇恨言论（种族主义、性别歧视、恐同、跨性别恐惧、宗教歧视）
- 持续纠缠（persistent sealioning）
- 冒充 OpenOBA 官方人员、赞助商或合作伙伴
- 利用 Issues/PRs 进行商业广告或推销

---

## 执行

社区领导者负责执行本行为准则，并将对违规行为采取适当的纠正措施。

### 执行级别

| 级别 | 措施 | 期限 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | 私下警告 + 撤回内容 | 一次性 |
| 2 | 公开警告 + 临时禁言 | 30-90 天 |
| 3 | 永久禁言 + 公开通知 | 永久 |
| 4 | 移出项目 + 拒绝贡献 | 永久 |

### 申诉

被处罚的个人可在收到处罚通知后 30 天内通过 **postmaster@openoba.com** 提交书面申诉。由与案件无关联的至少 3 名资深社区成员组成的独立审查小组将进行审查。审查决定为最终决定。

---

## 举报

举报违规行为请联系：**postmaster@openoba.com**

请包括以下信息：
- 违规者的 GitHub 用户名或身份
- 时间、平台及链接（Issue/PR/评论 URL）
- 违规内容的截图或文字副本
- 任何证人信息
- 您的联系方式（接受匿名举报）

所有举报将：
- 在 24 小时内确认收到
- 在 7 个工作日内进行评估
- 在 30 天内解决
