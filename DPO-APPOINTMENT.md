### 三、DPO 职责

依 **PIPL 第 52 条 / GDPR Article 38-39**，DPO 的主要职责：

1. **信息与咨询**：向公司、员工、用户、监管机构**提供**有关个人信息保护法律合规的**信息与建议**；
2. **合规监督**：**监督**公司个人信息处理活动的合规性；
3. **政策制定**：参与**制定**个人信息保护政策、标准操作规程（SOP）；
4. **影响评估**：**主持**个人信息保护影响评估（PIPIA / DPIA）；
5. **培训**：**组织**员工个人信息保护培训；
6. **联络监管**：**作为公司与个人信息保护监管机构的联络人**；
7. **风险预警**：发现违规风险时**及时报告**公司管理层；
8. **事件响应**：**主持**个人信息泄露事件的内部调查与监管报告；
9. **数据主体权利响应**：**处理**用户行使数据主体权利的请求；
10. **审计与认证**：**协调**外部审计与个人信息保护认证。

### 四、DPO 独立性保障

依 **GDPR Article 38(3)**：

1. DPO **不因履职而被解雇或处罚**；
2. DPO **不直接参与**决定个人信息处理活动的业务运营（**利益冲突回避**）；
3. DPO **直接向公司最高管理层（CEO / 董事会）** 报告工作；
4. DPO 享有**充分资源**（独立预算、专门团队、办公设施）履职；
5. DPO **不接收**与其履职职责冲突的指示。


### 六、个人信息泄露应急响应

```
发现或被告知个人信息泄露
  ↓
DPO 立即启动应急响应（2h 内）
  ↓
评估影响范围：涉及数据种类、用户数量、敏感度
  ↓
若涉及 100 万用户以上 / 包含敏感个人信息 / 长期未发现
  ├─ 72 小时内向 PIPL 监管机构报告
  ├─ 72 小时内通知欧盟 EDPB（GDPR Art 33）
  └─ 24 小时内通知受影响用户（PIPL 第 57 条 / GDPR Art 34）
  ↓
若不达上述门槛
  └─ 内部记录 + 30 日内报告
  ↓
内部调查、修复、归档
```

### 七、年度合规报告

DPO 应当**每年 6 月**向公司董事会提交**年度个人信息保护合规报告**，包含：

1. 当年个人信息处理活动统计（处理量、用户类别、数据种类）；
2. 用户数据主体权利请求统计（响应时间、争议案件）；
3. 个人信息泄露事件统计（数量、严重度、修复情况）；
4. 个人信息保护影响评估（PIPIA / DPIA）开展情况；
5. 监管检查与处罚情况；
6. 下一年度合规工作计划与预算。

### 3. DPO Responsibilities

Per **PIPL Art 52 / GDPR Art 38-39**, the DPO's primary responsibilities include:

1. **Information and consultation**: Provide information and advice to the Company, employees, users, and supervisory authorities on PIPL/GDPR compliance;
2. **Compliance monitoring**: Monitor the Company's personal information processing activities for compliance;
3. **Policy development**: Participate in formulating personal information protection policies and SOPs;
4. **Impact assessment**: Lead Personal Information Protection Impact Assessments (PIPIA / DPIA);
5. **Training**: Organize employee personal information protection training;
6. **Regulatory liaison**: Act as the liaison between the Company and personal information protection supervisory authorities;
7. **Risk warning**: Promptly report compliance risk to senior management;
8. **Incident response**: Lead internal investigations of personal information breach incidents and regulatory reporting;
9. **Data subject rights response**: Handle user requests to exercise data subject rights;
10. **Audit and certification**: Coordinate external audits and personal information protection certification.

### 4. DPO Independence Safeguards

Per **GDPR Article 38(3)**:

1. The DPO **shall not be dismissed or penalized for performing DPO tasks**;
2. The DPO **shall not be directly involved in** determining business operations of personal information processing (conflict of interest avoidance);
3. The DPO **reports directly to** senior management (CEO / Board);
4. The DPO has **adequate resources** (independent budget, dedicated team, office facilities) to perform duties;
5. The DPO **shall not receive** instructions conflicting with DPO duties.

### 5. Data Subject Contact Procedure

```
User exercises data subject right (access/rectify/erase/portability/object)
  ↓
Email postmaster@openoba.com
  ↓
DPO team acknowledges within 7 business days
  ↓
Verify user identity (anti-impersonation)
  ↓
Process within 30 days (extendable to 60 days for complex cases, with 7-day prior notice)
  ↓
Written response to user
  ↓
Archive for 3 years
```

### 6. Personal Information Breach Emergency Response

```
Discovery or notification of personal information breach
  ↓
DPO initiates emergency response (within 2h)
  ↓
Assess impact scope: data types, user count, sensitivity
  ↓
If 1M+ users / sensitive personal info / long-undiscovered
  ├─ Report to PIPL supervisory authority within 72 hours
  ├─ Notify EU EDPB within 72 hours (GDPR Art 33)
  └─ Notify affected users within 24 hours (PIPL Art 57 / GDPR Art 34)
  ↓
If below threshold
  └─ Internal record + report within 30 days
  ↓
Internal investigation, remediation, archive
```

### 7. Annual Compliance Report

The DPO shall submit an **Annual Personal Information Protection Compliance Report** to the Board of Directors **every June**, including:

1. Annual personal information processing statistics (volume, user categories, data types);
2. User data subject rights request statistics (response time, dispute cases);
3. Personal information breach statistics (count, severity, remediation);
4. PIPIA / DPIA execution status;
5. Supervisory inspections and penalties;
6. Next-year compliance work plan and budget.

### 8. Amendments

Any **change** to this notice (including DPO personnel change, contact change, duty adjustment) will be **announced at least 30 days in advance** on the official website.

### 9. Governing Law

This notice is **governed by the laws of the People's Republic of China**. For EU users, **GDPR also applies**.
