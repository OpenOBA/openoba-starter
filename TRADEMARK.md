### 五、域名争议

5.1 公司保留通过 **UDRP（统一域名争议解决政策）**、**中国国际经济贸易仲裁委员会域名争议解决中心**、**亚洲域名争议解决中心 (ADNDRC)** 等渠道解决商标抢注的权利。

5.2 争议解决方式：
- （1）协商转让（5 个工作日响应期）；
- （2）协商不成 → UDRP 或 ADNDRC 仲裁（费用由抢注方承担）；
- （3）仲裁裁决 → 强制转移 / 注销域名。

### 七、侵权举报

如发现商标侵权行为，请发送证据至 **postmaster@openoba.com**：

- （1）侵权方主体信息（公司名 / 个人姓名 / 联系方式）；
- （2）侵权证据（截图、链接、商品图、域名 WHOIS）；
- （3）侵权性质（假冒 / 抢注 / 不正当竞争）；
- （4）举报人联系方式（可匿名）。

**处理时效**：5 个工作日内首次响应；30 个工作日内完成评估；**严重案件**（涉及消费者权益）启动 24 小时加急流程。

### 八、保护措施

公司对商标采取以下保护措施：

- 商标监测：与专业商标监测公司合作，**每月**出具监测报告；
- 海关备案：核心商标在**中国海关知识产权备案系统**备案；
- 平台投诉：在 GitHub、Twitter/X、抖音、微信公众号、淘宝、京东、亚马逊等平台建立**官方投诉快速通道**；
- 域名保护：注册 openoba.com / .cn / .com.cn / .io / .ai / .org / .net / .dev 等主流 TLD。

### 5. Domain Disputes

5.1 The Company reserves the right to resolve trademark squatting through **UDRP (Uniform Domain-Name Dispute-Resolution Policy)**, **CIETAC Domain Name Dispute Resolution Center**, and **ADNDRC (Asian Domain Name Dispute Resolution Centre)**.

5.2 Dispute resolution process:
- (1) Negotiated transfer (5 business day response period);
- (2) If negotiation fails → UDRP or ADNDRC arbitration (costs borne by the squatter);
- (3) Arbitration award → forced transfer / cancellation of domain.

### 6. Trademark License Application Process

To obtain a trademark license (outside "permitted use" scenarios):

```
Applicant emails postmaster@openoba.com
  ↓
  Subject: Trademark License Application
  Content: Applicant info / Trademark use scenario / Business plan / Contact
  ↓
  Company responds within 5 business days
  ↓
  Approval → Sign TRADEMARK-LICENSE.md
  Rejection → Written explanation of reasons
```

### 7. Infringement Reporting

Send infringement evidence to **postmaster@openoba.com**:

- (1) Infringer's entity information (company name / individual name / contact);
- (2) Infringement evidence (screenshots, links, product images, domain WHOIS);
- (3) Infringement nature (counterfeiting / squatting / unfair competition);
- (4) Reporter's contact information (anonymous reports accepted).

**Processing SLA**: 5 business days for first response; 30 business days for assessment; **serious cases** (involving consumer rights) trigger 24-hour expedited process.

### 8. Protection Measures

The Company takes the following protection measures:

- **Trademark monitoring**: partnered with professional trademark monitoring firms; **monthly** monitoring reports;
- **Customs recordation**: core trademarks recorded with **PRC Customs Intellectual Property Recordation System**;
- **Platform complaints**: established **official expedited complaint channels** on GitHub, Twitter/X, Douyin, WeChat Official Accounts, Taobao, JD, Amazon, etc.;
- **Domain protection**: registered openoba.com / .cn / .com.cn / .io / .ai / .org / .net / .dev and other major TLDs.

### 9. Amendments

Amendments to this policy will be announced on our website **at least 30 days in advance**.

### 10. Governing Law
